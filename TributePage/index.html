Elon Musk

The entrepreneur who achieved the impossible.

Here's a time line of Elon Musk life:

  • 2002 Musk founded Space Exploration Technologies Corp
  • 2004Musk led the Series A round of investment in February, joining Tesla's board of directors as its chairman.
  • 2015Musk announced the creation of OpenAI
  • 2016 Musk co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology start-up company to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence
  • 2017 while stuck in traffic, Musk tweeted "[I] am going to build a tunnel boring machine and just start digging ..." The company was named 'The Boring Company' (TBC)
  • 2018SpaceX launches the Falcon Heavy rocket; Tesla reaches its goal of producing 5,000 Model 3s per week.
  • 2019Starship spacecraft prototype unveiled; Tesla announces plans for Gigafactory in Europe.
  • 2020SpaceX Crew Dragon successfully carries astronauts to the International Space Station; Tesla's market cap surpasses major car manufacturers.
  • 2021 Neuralink demonstrates a monkey controlling a computer with its brain; Tesla begins accepting Bitcoin as payment.
  • 2022 SpaceX Starship completes its first orbital flight; Tesla achieves record vehicle deliveries in a single quarter.
  • 2023Elon Musk outlines plans for a Mars colony; Neuralink conducts human clinical trials for brain-computer interface.
